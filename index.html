What is carbon budget?
The carbon budget is the estimated amount of carbon dioxide the world can emit while still hacing a likely chance of limiting global temperture rise to 2 degree above pre-industrial levels. The international scientific community estimates this budget to be 3 trillion tones of carbon dioxide, i.e. 1000PgC
