WHAT HAPPENS IF WE BLOW THE BUDGET?
If emissions continue unabated, global sea level could be nearly 1 meter higher by 2100.

The intensity and frequency of wildfires will increase throughout the world with each degree of warming.

Heavy precipation events will likely increase over some land areas.

The duration and intensity of droughts may increase in many regions.

What is carbon budget?
The carbon budget is the estimated amount of carbon dioxide the world can emit while still hasing a likely chance of limiting global temperture rise to 2℃ above pre-industrial levels. The international scientific community estimates this budget to be 3 trillion tones of carbon dioxide, i.e. 1000PgC.
